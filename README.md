# Digital Literacy Project 

Welcome to my **Digital Literacy project** repository!  
This project was completed as part of the **CSE0001 – Digital Literacy** subject at **VIT Bhopal University**.

---

##  About the Project

This project covers five practical tasks that helped me understand important digital skills, digital safety, professional communication, and the use of online platforms. The tasks include creating visual content, building digital profiles, exploring online tools, practising professional communication, and studying a cybersecurity topic.

---

##  Project Tasks Overview

###  **Task 1 – Digital Infographic**
I created a digital infographic using Canva that explains what digital literacy is, why it’s important, and key online safety practices.

###  **Task 2 – Digital Profiles**
I set up and updated my profiles on:
- GitHub  
- LinkedIn  
- Kaggle  
to build my professional digital presence.

###  **Task 3 – Coding Practice & Google Form**
I solved a beginner-level problem on a coding platform and created a Google Form titled *“Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz”* to test basic digital literacy concepts.

###  **Task 4 – Email Etiquette & Social Media**
I wrote two professional emails and created a checklist of social media do’s and don’ts to demonstrate appropriate communication skills.

###  **Task 5 – Cybercrime Case Study**
I completed a case study on a phishing attack to learn about how cybercrime works and how to prevent it.
